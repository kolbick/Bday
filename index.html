Our Staff

Meet the people who keep Changing Tides running every day.
Aubrey Dunning, MS in Professional Counseling, LCAS-A
Executive Director, Board Member

Today the staff and clients at Changing Tides are quietly hijacking this space for a different kind of update: a birthday message for Aubrey, who is “officially 25 again” and still somehow managing more moving parts than the rest of us can even see.

Aubrey is the person who always seems to be on the phone with someone who needs something — a nervous intake, a worried family member, a client in a hard moment, or a staff member who just needs backup. She lives in the middle of the fires and still finds a way to keep everyone else steady.

Most people only see the calm surface. We see the reality underneath: she’s constantly putting out fires, juggling schedules, solving problems that never hit the group room, and making sure clients and staff feel supported instead of alone. When things get messy, she doesn’t disappear — she leans in.

On this “25th” birthday, we want her to hear this clearly: the work she does here matters more than she knows. Clients feel it. Families feel it. Staff feel it. This place does not run without her, and we are grateful for the way she shows up for every one of us, every day.

Happy Birthday, Aubrey — from your staff and your clients. We’re all better off because you keep answering the phone.
